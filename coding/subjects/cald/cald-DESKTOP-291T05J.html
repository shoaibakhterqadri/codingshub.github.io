1. Introduction to Computer Architecture & Logic Design


1) Write a program to take three integers and add and display them.

Output:


Source Code:

.data
prompt: .asciiz "Enter three integers \n"
sum_msg: .asciiz "The sum is "
.text
.globl main
main:
la $a0,prompt
li $v0,4
syscall
li $v0,5
syscall
move $t1,$v0
li $v0,5
syscall
move $t2,$v0
li $v0,5
syscall
move $t3,$v0
addu $t1,$t1,$t2
addu $t1,$t1,$t3
la $a0,sum_msg
li $v0,4
syscall
move $a0,$t1
li $v0,1
syscall
li $v0,10
syscall
